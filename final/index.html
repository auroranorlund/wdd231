Welcome

Starry Night, Vincent Van Gogh, 1889

The Fine Art Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping the general population develop a greater appreciation for fine art outside of museums. While its primary focus is on paintings, all forms of fine art are welcomed by the Foundation.

Created by a group of college students during their freshman year, the Fine Art Foundation has since matured into a flourishing network of artists, critics, and general enjoyers of art. Our work and messages have been featured in Times, Good Morning America, and other popular media.

Our mission is to help the individual find the beauty of human creativity and ignite the spark of inspiration within them. We are a strictly anti-generative-AI organization. AI "art" never has been and never will be art, and is not welcome in this space.

White Water Lilies, Claude Monet, 1899

Most of the content featured by the Fine Art Foundation will be public domain works due to copyright constraints. However, we love featuring the work of our members. If you would like to have your works featured on our website or social media, please contact us at info@fineartfoundation.com. Submissions for the painting of the month must be traditional paintings such as acrylic, oil, watercolor, etc. Digital submissions will not be considered, but may be featured on our social media or elsewhere.
